Ordering

How can I track my order?

We will email you as items in your order ship, or if there are updates on the status of your order. If you have a Nordstrom.com profile, sign in to your account. You'll see which purchases are on the way and get to the shipment tracking updates. If you don't have an account, find your order number, then go to order information and enter it. For international orders, please call 1.319.846.4140 or check your order history on Borderfree.

How do I know if ordering online is secure?

To help determine if your connection is secure, look at the status bar of your browser window. If you see an unbroken key or a closed lock (depending on your browser), Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) should be active, which helps to protect the transmission of information through the website. You should also verify that the URL for the page you are on includes "https" at the beginning. Most browsers offer additional security alerts as well.

What happens when my item is backordered?

Items that aren't available to ship when an order is placed are on backorder and will ship at a later date. We'll provide you with an approximate ship date when you add the item to your bag and when you check out. Backorder ship dates are approximate, and we'll email you if the date changes. Please note that, while your credit card will be authorized on the date you place your order, you will not be charged until the item ships.


Account Updates as of April 2021

Separate Nordstrom and Nordstrom Rack accounts that use the same email address will soon be combined into a single account. Your credentials, name, contact information, payment methods, shipping addresses and Nordy Club account will all be available under one account.

What happens if I only have a Nordstrom account?

If you only have an account on either Nordstrom.com or Nordstromrack.com, you will now be able to sign in to the other site using your existing credentials. Changes made to your account will apply to both sites, making it easier to manage your shopping experience.

What happens if I have an account on Nordstrom and Nordstrom Rack and I use the same email address on both?

If you have accounts on both Nordstrom.com and Nordstromrack.com using the same email address to sign in, the account you use less often will be merged with your other account, and the remaining account will be ready to use across both sites. You should receive an email from us informing you of the sign-in you'll be using. You can use your account on both sites, making it easier than ever to manage your shopping experience. Plus, if you're a member of The Nordy Club, your in-store and online purchase information will show up under your purchase history.

What happens if I have an account on Nordstrom and Nordstrom Rack but I use different email addresses for each one?

If you have accounts on both Nordstrom.com and Nordstromrack.com with separate email addresses as your sign-in, you will continue to sign in to each site as usual using the email address you've provided for each one.

I can't find my Nordstrom purchase history. What happened to it?

Information for past Nordstrom orders may no longer be viewable in your account purchase history. If you need to find information regarding a past Nordstrom purchase, we recommend referencing your order confirmation emails or contacting our Customer Care team via chat or by phone at 1.888.282.6060.
